HOW THE PROCESS WORKS

You can submit a wide range of commercial lending deals including property, development, and business funding opportunities.

In many cases, we can issue a Letter of Offer within 48 hours once the deal has been reviewed and the required information has been received.

While having documents ready helps speed up the process, we can guide you on what is required during the submission stage.

Funding amounts vary depending on the project, borrower profile, and lending structure.

Settlement timelines vary depending on the complexity of the deal and documentation requirements.
