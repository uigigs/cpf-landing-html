Asset Backed Lending in Australia

Asset backed lending is a type of finance where a loan is secured against property or other tangible assets. It is commonly used by property developers and investors who require fast access to capital for development projects, construction funding, or short-term bridging requirements.

Compared to traditional bank lending, asset backed finance often allows for faster approval times and more flexible structures, making it a popular solution for developers working on small to medium sized housing projects.

Why Developers Use Asset Backed Lending

  • Faster approvals than traditional bank finance
  • Flexible loan structures for property development deals
  • Funding secured by property assets
  • Suitable for complex or time-sensitive projects
  • Funding available for small to medium sized developments

When Asset Backed Lending is Used

Residential Developments

Funding for small to medium housing developments including townhouse and duplex projects.

Residual Stock Loans

Loans secured against unsold apartments or townhouses following project completion.

Land Subdivision

Funding for subdivision projects where property value supports the loan.

Bridging Finance

Short-term loans designed to provide quick access to capital while a longer-term solution is arranged.
